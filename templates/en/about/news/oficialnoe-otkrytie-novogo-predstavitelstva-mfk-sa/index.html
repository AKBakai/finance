Official opening of a new representative office of the MFC "Salym Finance" in Kochkor-Ata.

Opening of a new representative office in  Kochkor-Ata town.

In Kochkor-Ata town opened a new representative office of the MFC "Salym Finance".

In the new representative office, you can receive a consultation on the following services:

-Deposits;

-Credits;

-Bank guarantees.

The first 15 customers of the branch are waiting for pleasant gifts!

We are waiting for you at:

Kochkor-Ata town, st. Gagarin, 2/1.

Working hours: 8:30 a.m. - 4:30 p.m.

Phone: +996 (550) 781521

Saturday: 9:00 a.m. - 1:00 p.m.

Sunday: Non-working day.

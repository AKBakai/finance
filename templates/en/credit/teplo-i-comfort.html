Credit "Heat and comfort"

Do you want to insulate your home? Do you want to cross over to energy-saving types of heating or add a comfortable bathroom to the house? Do you want to replace windows and doors? With the loan "Warmth and comfort" from Salym Finance MFC your plans will come true!

Loan amount
up to 500 ths. soms
Interest rate(annual)
27.9% in soms
Apply for a loan Calculate loan

The loan for:

Installation of heating system

Installation of windows and doors

Installation of water supply and bathrooms

Thermal insulation of walls

Credit terms and conditions

  • Credit amount: Without collateral, on the surety from 50 ths. soms to 500 ths. soms / $ USD equivalent;
  • Credit term: from 3 months to 36 months;
  • Age of the borrower: from 22 years to 75 years;
  • Interest rate: 27.9% in soms / 16.9% in US dollars ;
  • Effective interest rate including sales tax: KGS - from 33.94%; USD - from 19.93%;
  • Commission: KGS - 1% // USD - 3.5% of the credit amount;
  • The ability to provide a flexible schedule;
  • Early loan repayment without warnings and commissions!
